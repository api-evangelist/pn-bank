# P&N Bank

> P&N Bank is the retail banking brand of Police & Nurses Limited (ABN 69 087 651 876, AFSL/ACL 240701), a customer-owned (mutual) bank headquartered in Perth and one of Western Australia's largest locally based banks. As an Authorised Deposit-taking Institution it participates in Australia's Consumer Data Right (CDR / Open Banking), exposing a public, unauthenticated Product Reference Data (PRD) API built to the DSB Consumer Data Standards.

## APIs

- [P&N Bank CDR Product Reference Data API](https://www.pnbank.com.au/help-and-support/open-banking/pn-bank-products-api/): Public, unauthenticated product catalogue. Base URL https://public.cdr-api.pnbank.com.au/cds-au/v1/banking/products. Requires the x-v header. Two operations: listBankingProducts and getBankingProductDetail.
